Recent Advances in Vision-and-Language Research

CVPR 2020 Tutorial

Location: TBD

Overview

This tutorial is pivoted on understanding, reasoning, and generation over multimodalities, covering state-of-the-art approaches and principles in recent popular Vision-and-Language tasks, including visual captioning, visual grounding, visual question answering and reasoning, text-to-image generation, and self-supervised learning for universal image-text representations.
