Overview

In this paper, we present our CVPR tutorial proposal on Recent Advances in Vision-and-Language (V+L) Research. The tutorial is pivoted on understanding, reasoning, and generation over multimodalities, covering state-of-the-art approaches and principles in recent popular V+L tasks, includingvisualcaptioning,visualgrounding,visualquestion answering and reasoning, text-to-image generation, and self-supervised learning for universal image-text representations.
